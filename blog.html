Best Time to Visit Himachal

Snow lovers: Dec–Feb. Flower valleys: Mar–Jun. Clear skies and apple season: Sep–Oct.

Top 5 Adventures

Paragliding (Bir), rafting (Kullu), trekking (Spiti), skiing (Solang), camping (Tirthan).
